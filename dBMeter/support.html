Common questions

  • Why does dB Meter need microphone access? It measures ambient sound levels in real time. Audio is analyzed on your device only and is never recorded or sent anywhere.
  • How accurate are the readings? dB Meter uses your iPhone’s built-in microphone, so readings are approximate. You can fine-tune them with the calibration offset in Settings.
  • How do I restore my purchase? Open Settings in the app and tap “Restore Purchase.”
